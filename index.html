featured places

Jaipur, India

Jaipur is a regal paradise of culture and legacy, rich with architectural jewels, and is affectionately dubbed "The Pink City" for the pink color of its old structures.

Darjeeling, India

Nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas, Darjeeling is a serene hill station famed for its lush tea gardens, stunning vistas, and vibrant culture.

Goa, India

Bask in the sun-kissed shores and vibrant culture of Goa, India's coastal gem.

Kolkata, India

Discover the soul of India in Kolkata, a city pulsating with history, culture, and a rich tapestry of flavors.

Kanyakumari

This coastal town is renowned for its stunning sunrises and sunsets, painting the sky in a riot of colors.

Delhi, India

Delhi, the dynamic heart of India, is a mesmerizing blend of history, culture, and modernity. Explore the ancient wonders of Old Delhi.
